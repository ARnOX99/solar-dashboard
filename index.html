System Overview

Arduino Uno controls sensors and motors; ESP8266 handles WiFi and ThingSpeak communication.

Sensors include BH1750 (light intensity), LDRs for directional light sensing, INA219 for current measurement, and voltage sensing on ESP analog input.

Servos adjust the solar panelâ€™s position based on sensor data. Power management includes two batteries with relay switching.

About Project

The dual-axis solar tracking system improves energy harvest by continuously adjusting the panel to face the sun. It leverages real-time sensor data, cloud monitoring, and remote control to optimize performance and reliability.
