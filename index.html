Solar Tracker

IoT-Based Dual-Axis Solar Tracking and Energy Monitoring System

Abstract

The rapid increase in global energy demand and the limitations of conventional fixed-position solar panels have emphasized the need for intelligent solar tracking systems. This project presents an IoT-based dual-axis solar tracking and energy monitoring system designed to maximize solar energy harvesting efficiency while enabling real-time monitoring and control.

The system utilizes a dual-axis tracking mechanism driven by servo motors to dynamically align the solar panel in both horizontal and vertical directions based on sunlight intensity. A BH1750 digital light sensor provides accurate ambient light measurements, while multiple LDR sensors assist in detecting directional light imbalance for precise tracking error correction.

Electrical performance parameters such as voltage and current are continuously monitored using an INA219 power sensor. Solar energy is stored in dual lithium-ion batteries through a controlled charging module. An ESP8266 Wi-Fi module enables seamless data transmission to the ThingSpeak IoT platform. A custom web dashboard provides live monitoring, historical analysis, alerts, and intelligent power management features.
